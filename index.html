About Me

As a psychology graduate, I have always been fascinated by how the human mind works and how we can leverage this knowledge to design better experiences for people. I am also passionate about technology and the limitless possibilities it offers for creativity and innovation.

Combining my interests in psychology and technology, I have found my niche in designing and building websites that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and intuitive. I believe that understanding human behavior and cognitive processes is key to creating digital products that truly meet people's needs and desires.

Whether it's conducting user research, developing wireframes, or coding a website, I approach every project with a curious and analytical mindset, always striving to push the boundaries of what is possible. I am excited about the potential for technology to make a positive impact on people's lives, and I am eager to continue exploring the intersection of psychology and technology in my career.
